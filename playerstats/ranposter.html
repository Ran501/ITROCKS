Choney Rangdel is a maestro on the pitch, renowned for his extraordinary dribbling ability and quick acceleration. He effortlessly glides past defenders with a low center of gravity, making it look almost effortless. His vision and creativity allow him to deliver precise passes and set up teammates for goals, while his clinical finishing makes him a constant threat in front of the net. Rangdel's ability to read the game and anticipate plays sets him apart, making him not just a prolific scorer but also a brilliant playmaker.
